The Coca-Cola Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance, allowing for in-depth analysis and informed decision-making. Key utilities include:

- Visualizing sales trends: The dashboard uses charts and graphs to clearly illustrate sales data for different beverage brands and regions.
- Identifying key performance indicators (KPIs): The dashboard highlights important metrics such as total sales, units sold, price per unit, operating profit, and operating margin.
- Analyzing sales by state: The map allows for a geographical analysis of sales performance across the United States.
- Identifying influential factors: The dashboard helps to identify factors that influence operating profit, such as beverage brand and specific states.
- Making data-driven decisions: The insights provided by the dashboard can be used to optimize marketing strategies, improve product distribution, and enhance overall business performance.
